Questions & answers

Do I need to already have a domain?

Nope. If you don't have one, I'll help you buy it (usually $10-15/year). If you do, I'll put the site on your existing domain.

What if I don't like the demo?

No problem. You keep the link as a reference, and there's no charge or obligation. Most people like what they see, but there's zero pressure.

How long does it take?

Demo: 24-48 hours after you submit the form. Going live: within a week of approval, usually faster.

Are there any monthly fees?

Absolutely not. $250 deposit to start, $250 on completion. Your only ongoing cost is domain renewal (~$10-15/year) if you want to keep the site.

Can I make changes after it's live?

Yes. I include 30 days of small edits after launch. After that, changes are available at a reasonable hourly rate.

Do you work with businesses outside Tennessee?

Yes, but I specialize in Tennessee contractors and tradespeople. I understand the local market and what your customers are searching for.
